Our Tours

1. Historical Center Tour (2 hours)

Immerse yourself in the vibrant streets of Cartagena, where every corner tells a story of pirates, conquests, and ancient legends. Discover its colorful colonial facades, filled with art and culture, while marveling at the stunning churches, plazas, and flower-adorned balconies that seem straight out of a fairytale. Experience the magic of walking through a city that is a world heritage site, where every step takes you back in time amidst a tropical atmosphere that will leave you breathless.

2. City Tour

Includes:

  • Entrance to the Convent of La Popa (the best view of the city)
  • Photo stop at the San Felipe de Barajas Castle
  • Walk through the Walled City (optional shopping)

3. Palenque Tour

Discover the first free Palenque in the Americas, a living treasure of African culture in the heart of Colombia. Step into its streets full of history and pride, where ancestral traditions remain alive through music, dance, and vibrant colors decorating every corner. Enjoy its exquisite native cuisine, where African flavors blend with local ingredients to offer you an explosion of sensations. Immerse yourself in stories of resistance and freedom, told by the descendants of the enslaved who fought for their emancipation, in a place where past and present intertwine in a unique cultural experience that will transform you.

4. Visit to the Totumo Volcano

Immerse yourself in the warm, healing waters of the medicinal mud volcano, a unique natural wonder just minutes from Cartagena. Let the rejuvenating properties of the mud envelop your body while you relax floating in its mysterious and thick texture. Experience an ancestral tradition that, according to local legend, not only revitalizes your skin but also balances body and mind. Enjoy a natural massage that only nature can offer, and take with you the feeling of well-being and renewal from the first contact.
